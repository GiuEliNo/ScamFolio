USER STORIES:
1. As a user, I want to check my actual balance.
2. As a user, I want to see the coin daily value.
3. As a user, I want to buy and sell my coins.
4. As a user, I want to see how the price of a coin changed in the last month.

FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS:
1. The app shall save all the informations about a personal balance.
2. The app shall display the updated currencies prices.
3. The app must allow the sale and purchase of coins.
4. The app shall display past values for a currency.
